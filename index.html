About domestic cats

They are oftem called house cats when kept as indoor pet or simply cats when there is no need to distinguish them from other felids and felines. they are often valued by humans for companionship

There are more than seventy cat breeds recognized by various cat relistries

Cat senses

  • They can hear sounds too high in frequency for human ears
  • They can see in near darkness
  • They have poorer color vision and better sense of smell than humans

History

Cats were venerated in ancien Egypt, but there may have been instances of domestication as early as the Neolithie from around 9,500 years ago (7500 BC).

The lions

Present in Africa and India, the lion typicallyinhabits grasslands and savannahs, but is absent in dense forests.Its is usuallymore diurnal than other big eats, but when persecuted adapts to being active at night and at twilight.
A lion pride consists of a few adult males, related females and cubs. Prides vary in size and composition from three to 20 adult lions, depending on habitat and prey availability.Females cooperate when hunting and prey mostly on large ungulates, including antelope, deer, buffalo, zebra and even giraffe
